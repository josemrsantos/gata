GATA'S POSITION AND EXPRESSION: Gata sits squarely in front of the chalkboard, facing the viewer directly, four paws on a grey metal desk. Her expression is composed, patient, and forensically unimpressed — the look of an investigator who has confirmed exactly what she suspected and finds no satisfaction in it. She does not gesture or point. She simply sits.
