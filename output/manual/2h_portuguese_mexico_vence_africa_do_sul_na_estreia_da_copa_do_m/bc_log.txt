**REVIEW 1 — Too obvious / too lenient?**
My first instinct was to show Portuguese café commentators comparing Mexico vs South Africa to Portugal's situation. That's too direct and expected. The sharper angle is to lean into the *mirror effect* — the Portuguese fan watches a game that has nothing to do with Portugal and somehow turns it into a coaching session for Rui Jorge, while the South Africans (hosts eliminated at home, as Portugal knows from 2004's emotional context) are the unspoken punchline that the Portuguese *almost* acknowledge but then pivot away from to talk about Ronaldo. The joke should make the audience laugh at themselves, not at Mexico or South Africa.
